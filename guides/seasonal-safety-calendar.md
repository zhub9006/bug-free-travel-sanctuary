## 🛡️ Emergency "DO NOT GO" Destinations and Seasons

| Timeframe | Region to AVOID Absolutely | Why? | Safe Alternative Pick |
|---|---|---|---|
| **All year** | **Amazon Basin** (Brazil/Peru/Colombia) | Mosquitoes every minute; malaria, dengue, yellow fever endemic | Madeira / Atacama deep desert interior |
| **May–Sep tropical** | **West/Central Africa** | Mosquitoes 24/7; severe malaria/dengue/chikungunya burden | Madagascar southern highlands only |
| **Dec–Mar** | **Caribbean tropical lowlands** | Mosquitoes every season; chikungunya on some islands | Madeira, Canary Islands at altitude (cooler) |
| **Nov–Mar (boreal summer)** | **Patagonia (Argentina/Chile)** | **Worst bug season on Earth for bug-sensitive travelers** — sandflies swarm endlessly near every river/lake | **Patagonia JUN–AUG (their winter) — superbly bug-free** |
| **May–Jul** | **Southern US (SE states)** + **Canadian boreal forests** | Mosquito peak; ticks ramping up | Pacific Northwest (cooler, drier, fewer bugs) |
| **Apr–Aug** | **Southeast Asia** | Dengue zone especially Thai/KH coastal lowlands | Japan Hokkaido winter (Dec–March) |
