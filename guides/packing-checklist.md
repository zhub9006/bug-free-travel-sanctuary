# 🛡️ Essential Bug-Avoid Packing Checklist

> Even on a "bug-free" trip, carry these for insurance against rare lapses.

- [ ] **DEET 30–50% repellent** or **Picaridin 20%**
- [ ] **Permethrin-treated clothing** (6+ wash durability)
- [ ] **Mosquito net** for accommodation (traveler safety net for rare lapses)
- [ ] **Antihistamine cream** (for unexpected bites)
- [ ] **Light-colored, long-sleeve convertible hiking pants**
- [ ] **Pre-treated gaiters** for tick-heavy transitional zones
- [ ] **Wasp/hornet spray** for urban markets & restaurants in temperate cities
- [ ] **Sunscreen SPF 30+** + sun hat (bug-free = often high UV exposure)
- [ ] **Lightweight head net** (worst-case unexpected swarms)

> **Philosophy:** Pack one step beyond comfort. Even in Antarctica, rare intruders happen. Better safe than itchy! 🏖️
